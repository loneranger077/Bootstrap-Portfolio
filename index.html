About Me

However, I have returned with a vengeance to tackle coding... I was born in Huntsville, AL (Rocket City FTW), but mainly was raised and currently reside in the Atlanta area. I grew up going to Georgia Tech football games at Bobby Dodd Stadium, so I am happy to be back on campus!
